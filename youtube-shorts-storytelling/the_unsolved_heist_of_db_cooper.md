# Script 2: The Unsolved Heist of D.B. Cooper

**Niche:** True Crime / Heist Mystery
**Content Type:** YouTube Short (approx. 45-60 seconds)
**Concept:** A daring skyjacker vanishes after parachuting from a Boeing 727 with a ransom of $200,000.

---

## Script:

**(0-5s) Hook:**
**Visuals:** Dramatic footage of a plane flying through the sky. Upbeat, daring heist-style music begins.
**Narration:** Imagine skyjacking a plane, demanding a fortune... then vanishing into thin air.

**(5-15s) The Daring Act:**
**Visuals:** A man in a suit (stock footage) looking determined. A graphic showing a plane interior.
**Narration:** On Thanksgiving Eve, 1971, a man calling himself Dan Cooper boarded a Boeing 727. He calmly handed a flight attendant a note: he had a bomb and demanded $200,000 and four parachutes.

**(15-30s) The Ransom and the Escape:**
**Visuals:** A briefcase being handed over. Graphics showing the plane flying. Then, a shot of the rear plane door opening.
**Narration:** The demand met, Cooper instructed the pilots to fly towards Mexico. Over southwestern Washington, in a storm, he opened the plane's aft stairs and leaped out of the rattling jet, never to be seen again.

**(30-45s) The Lingering Riddle:**
**Visuals:** Police searching woods, a muddy dollar bill, a grainy sketch of Cooper, a question mark graphic.
**Narration:** Despite a massive manhunt and decades of investigation, D.B. Cooper (his name likely misspelled) was never found, and most of the ransom money vanished with him. Was he a criminal mastermind, or did he meet his end in the wilderness?

**(45-60s) Call to Action/Lingering Question:**
**Visuals:** A parachute falling against a night sky. Music fades.
**Narration:** The legend of D.B. Cooper lives on. What do you think became of him? Drop your theories below!

---

## Visual/Audio Notes:
*   **Tone:** Adventurous, daring, mysterious.
*   **Narration Style:** Confident, slightly mysterious, engaging male voice.
*   **Visuals:** Stock footage: planes (interior/exterior), parachutes, forests, mountains, rain/storm effects, money graphics, briefcases, police searching, old newspaper clippings.
*   **Music:** Upbeat, suspenseful, heist-themed instrumental music. Something with a retro spy-movie feel.
